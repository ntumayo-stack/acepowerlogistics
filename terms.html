1. About ACE POWER LOGISTICS

ACE POWER LOGISTICS Ltd is a licensed customs clearance agent registered in Rwanda (TIN: 110993306), authorized by the Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA) to file import, export, transit, and other customs declarations on behalf of clients. Our services include customs brokerage, duty calculation, document preparation, and logistics advisory.

2. Scope of services

Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Preparation and filing of customs declarations on the RRA ASCYCUDA World system
  • Calculation of applicable duties, taxes, levies, and exemptions under RRA, EAC, COMESA, and AfCFTA frameworks
  • Advisory on HS code classification, import permits, and trade compliance
  • Coordination with freight forwarders, transporters, and government agencies
  • Indicative duty estimates via our online calculator tool (for informational purposes only)

The duty calculator available on our website provides indicative estimates only. Results are based on published RRA tariff schedules and standard EAC rates but do not constitute a binding quote or guarantee. Final duties and taxes are assessed exclusively by the Rwanda Revenue Authority at the time of filing, based on the actual declared value, HS code classification, and applicable trade agreements.

ACE POWER LOGISTICS shall not be held liable for any discrepancy between estimated and assessed duties.
