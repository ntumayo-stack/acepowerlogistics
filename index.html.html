EAC Common External Tariff

Rwanda applies 3 bands: 0% (raw materials & capital goods), 10% (intermediate goods), 25% (finished goods).

VAT on imports

18% VAT applies on most imported goods (CIF + duty). Some food & medical items are exempt.

EAC & COMESA exemptions

Goods from EAC or COMESA countries may qualify for 0% duty with a valid Certificate of Origin.
