Why use a licensed customs agent?

RRA requires all customs declarations to be filed by a licensed agent. Misclassification or undervaluation errors carry severe financial penalties.

We assign the correct HS code to every item — one mistake can cost 10× the price of this service.
We automatically identify EAC, AfCFTA and COMESA exemptions you are entitled to.
In red-channel cases, we manage the physical inspection and respond to RRA on your behalf.
You keep a full history of all your clearance files — useful for audits and accounting.
